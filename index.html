SipheLwande BnB

Siphe-Lwande BnB is in the town of Maclear, along the R396 route at 17 Ahrbeck Street. The street itself is very tranquil and quiet. We are within reach of the main town. There is ample parking within locked gates and cameras. There is a restaurant within 500m from the establishment. Siphe-Lwande BnB started operating in 2010. A gap was identified when a local hotel closed its doors. We started with only 2 bedrooms and now we operate with eleven bedrooms, a dining hall and a reception area. We have attained a three- star grading for the last four years. We have a vision to be a leading supplier of quality a ccommodation in Maclear, to supply a high standard conference facility. Siphe-Lwande Bed & Breakfast is a safe -haven for a short and long stay guests.

Siphe-Lwande BnB is located on the foothills of the Drakensburg Mountains in the Joe Gqabi Region within the Elundini Local Municipality of the Eastern Cape. Elundini Local Municipality comprises of the towns of Ugie, Maclear and Mount Fletcher. The area is largely an agricultural area with natural scenery: popular with rich fossils, dinosaur footprints and rock art.
